  • About

    Long is a researcher in the Performance Engineering Laboratory@UCD led by Prof. John Murphy.

    His research interests mainly includ parallel and distributed computing, large-scale data processing, Semantic Web and process mining. Specifically, he focuses on efficient large data processing in a way that uses current distributed computing platforms (e.g., HPC clusters and Cloud) to their fullest potential.
